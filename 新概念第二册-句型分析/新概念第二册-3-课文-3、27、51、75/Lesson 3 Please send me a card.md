Last summer, I went to Italy.

    - Last summer 前置时间状语

    - and

    - I sat in public gardens
      - I 主语
      - sat 谓语动词
      - in public gardens 谓语动词的地点状语

    - teach + sth. + sth. 教导 某人  某事

    - then + 句子

    - I did not understand a word

  - but

  - I did not send cards to my friends
